# Argus Product Context

Argus is a cryptographic black box and slashable mandate court for autonomous AI agents that control money.

## Users

DAO treasury operators, protocol teams, and crypto funds who want delegated AI execution without trusting prompt guardrails, centralized logs, or opaque backends.

## Product Purpose

Argus proves that an agent’s financial actions satisfied a mandate, or records why they did not, with on-chain consequences and replayable traces.

## Register

product

## Strategic Principles

- Rejection is the primary demo win.
- Every proof must be visible: tx hash, storage URI, trace root, verdict, and bond delta.
- The agent never executes directly. It proposes through ActionGate.
- Use mocks for DeFi targets, but keep mandate enforcement and slashing real.
- Store structured traces, not vague logs.

## Anti-References

- Generic AI firewall dashboard.
- Generic chatbot.
- Crypto casino neon UI.
- Contract interaction panel with no story.
- Plain shadcn scaffold.
